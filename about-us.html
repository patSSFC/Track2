Imagine a street art destination unlike any other in the world.

A landscape designed to be viewed in motion, with a wide array of large scale artworks racing alongside you. You’re racing some of the region’s and the planet’s greatest creative minds. This is a bus ride where the walls you pass are so interesting that you put your phone away. A train ride that sparks your imagination.

While mural projects around the country and around the world increase and grow, The SODO Track is the first to charge multiple artists to explore one theme side by side, and for that theme – motion, speed, progression – to reflect the experience of its viewers.

Artists from Seattle and around the country, together with international talent are painting large-scale murals to mark the portal to Downtown Seattle as an imaginative raceway of art in motion.

Our Team

4Culture

4Culture is a unique integration of the arts, heritage, preservation and public art committed to advancing community through culture. Public Art 4Culture, which commissions contemporary art for shared public space in King County, WA, is the convener and seed funder of The SODO Track.

Gage Hamilton

Gage is an artist and curator, and a co-organizer of Forest For The Trees, a nonprofit project dedicated to the creation of contemporary public art in Portland, OR. Commissioned by 4Culture, Gage has developed a Public Art Plan for The SODO Track and curated its first summer festival.

Tamar Benzikry

Tamar manages public art projects for 4Culture’s King County partners and consulting clients, championing artists in making their best work for each particular context. Tamar has led multiple new art initiatives with King County Metro Transit, and is the lead project manager for The SODO Track.

Urban Artworks

Urban Artworks is a Seattle-based nonprofit organization that provides opportunities for underserved youth and contemporary artists to work together to create public works of art. Originally established in SODO, and maker of the first murals along the SODO Urban Art Corridor, Urban Artworks’ youth engagement with SODO Track artists and the organization’s role as a founding SODO Track Partner, continue the legacy.

SODO BIA

Seattle, advocates for a safe, clean, and moving SODO. A founding SODO Track Partner, the SODO BIA has endorsed the Public Art Plan for the SODO Track and supports the wall preparation and maintenance of Track murals.

In a collaboration seeded by 4Culture, artist Gage Hamilton is working with the SODO BIA, Urban Artworks and Sound Transit to create The SODO Track.
